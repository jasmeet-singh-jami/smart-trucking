# Company Registration

At STS Inc we specialize in setting up a new corporation for you. You can choose
between a numbered corporation or a name corporation. Our consultants do the name
search for you and set your corporation for your HST also. Within few minutes your
corporation is all set to start business. We can also add a business name to your
existing corporation or request a MBL (Master Business License) for your corporation.

## OUR SERVICES

- Corporate name searches (NUANS®)
- Profit, non-profit, and charitable organization;
- Incorporation (ONTARIO and FEDERAL)
- Corporate minute books and seals
- Ontario corporate profile searches and certificates
- Ontario corporation amendment
- Corporation Dissolution
- HST Number Application
- Initial Notices/Notices of Change for Ontario Corporations
